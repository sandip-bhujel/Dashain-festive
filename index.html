Dashain is a major festival celebrated in Nepal and by Nepalese communities around the world.
It’s a vibrant and elaborate festival that usually lasts 15 days. It marks the victory of good over evil, commemorating the goddess Durga’s victory over the buffalo demon Mahishasura.During Dashain, families come together to enjoy elaborate feasts, exchange gifts, and perform various religious rituals. Houses are cleaned and decorated, and everyone gets new clothes. Kite flying is a popular activity, symbolizing the start of the festival.
